Tabletop RPG Projects

#Prestige Paths

Prestige Paths: Narrative First Advancement is a 24 page zine detailing new narrative driven advancement mechanics for d20 fantasy adventure games. It includes 20 new 'Paths' for players to explore.

It is available on Itch.io for $3 and there is also a free version with the first 6 paths.

#Minimum Character

Minimum Character is a little resource for adding character traits and bonds using a single page roll table.

It is available on Itch.io for free
